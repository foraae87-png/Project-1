How It Began

From a Home Kitchen to Yours

It started with a few extra lunch boxes. Neighbours who tasted the food asked for more, then their friends, then whole families. What was once a home kitchen cooking for loved ones quietly became a cloud kitchen trusted by hundreds.

The recipes haven't changed — they're the same ones passed down through generations, made the way amma and paati made them. No shortcuts, no artificial taste, just honest ingredients and patient cooking.

That promise sits at the heart of every meal we send out: it should taste like it came from someone's home, because it did.

Our Mission

Real Food for Real People

We believe everyone deserves a meal that feels personal — not mass-produced, not reheated, not forgotten. So we cook in small batches, fresh each morning, and deliver it while it's still warm.

Whether it's a working professional who misses home food, a family that wants one less thing to worry about, or an office that wants to feed its team well — we're here to make homemade food effortless.
